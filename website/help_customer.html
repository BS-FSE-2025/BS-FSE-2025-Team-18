Help Page

Welcome to our renovation project management site! Below are detailed instructions on how to navigate and use the various features of our site.

  • Home Page

    Start here to access all features of the website and view an overview of the site.

  • Product Catalog

    Explore and filter through our wide range of products for your renovation needs.

  • Cart

    View and manage the items you have added to your cart, and save your cart as a project in "My Works."

  • My Works

    Manage and view your saved projects, share them to the gallery, or delete them.

  • Checklist

    Organize tasks and keep track of your project progress. Update statuses like "Pending," "In Progress," or "Completed."

  • Gallery

    Explore renovation projects shared by other users. Filter by budget or browse freely for inspiration.

Additional Features

Profile Menu: Access profile settings, logout, and toggle accessibility options.

Accessibility Tools: Adjust font sizes for better readability.
